# AItitude

**The AI Is The Limit**

A premium AI show filmed at altitude in the Gulf's most iconic settings. Conversations with the builders, leaders, scientists, artists, and operators who are reshaping the world through AI.

## Formats

- **The Brief** (weekly) — What happened in AI this week. Fast takes, real implications.
- **The Builder** (monthly) — One guest who built something extraordinary with AI.
- **The Principal** (bimonthly) — C-suite, ministers, sovereign wealth operators.

## Filming Locations

- Atmosphere Restaurant, Burj Khalifa (122nd floor)
- Museum of the Future, Dubai
- Burj Al Arab Skyview Bar
- Address Sky View Pool
- Etihad Towers Observation Deck, Abu Dhabi
- Emirates First Class Suite
