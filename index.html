The Premise

There is no premium AI interview show anchored in the Gulf, filmed in extraordinary settings, covering the full range of human endeavour. AItitude fills that gap.

Every episode is filmed where decisions happen: at the top of the world's tallest buildings, aboard first class suites, inside the private rooms where the future is being shaped. The setting is not a backdrop — it is a co-host.
